Admission Process

Follow these steps to apply for our B.Sc Data Science program.

1

Online Application

Complete the online application form with your personal and academic details.

2

Document Upload

Upload required documents including academic transcripts and identification.

3

Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview with faculty members.

4

Admission Offer

Selected students will receive an admission offer letter via email.

5

Fee Payment

Complete the admission process by paying the fees and submitting required documents.

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualification

10+2 or equivalent with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.

Minimum Marks

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 10+2 (50% for reserved categories).

Interview Performance

Satisfactory performance in the personal interview.

Required Documents

Academic Records

10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates.

Identification

Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Passport.

Photographs

Recent passport-sized photographs.

Category Certificate

For reserved category students (if applicable).

Transfer Certificate

From the last institution attended.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various scholarships to recognize and support talented students.

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% tuition fee waiver

For students with exceptional academic performance in 10+2 (above 90%).

Eligibility Criteria:

Based on 10+2 marks.

Women in STEM Scholarship

Special scholarship to encourage female students to pursue data science education.

Eligibility Criteria:

Available for female candidates with good academic records.

Financial Need-based Aid

Support for economically disadvantaged students who demonstrate academic potential.

Eligibility Criteria:

Based on family income and academic merit.

Sports Excellence Scholarship

For students who have represented at state or national level in sports.

Eligibility Criteria:

Based on sports achievements and continued participation.
