Our Journey & Leadership

Nirmiti Degree College of Commerce, Arts and Science was founded in 2020 with a vision to provide exceptional higher education and foster innovation. Under the esteemed leadership of our Chairman, Mr. Raj Pandit, we have quickly established ourselves as a center for academic excellence.

Our commitment is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values necessary to thrive in a dynamic global environment. We focus on holistic development, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning.
